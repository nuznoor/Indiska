Sustainability

Did you know it takes 700 gallons of water to make one shirt?

How did we make sure this product was produced sustainably?

Made with Organic Cotton

Organic cotton is grown without chemical pesticides, fertilizers and genetically modified organisms (GMOs). About 80 % of the organic cotton is rain-fed thus requires less watering than conventional cotton, which reduces the pressure on local water sources.



We Saved Water

We used organic cotton and that helped us waste less water. On average, organic cotton saves 1,982 gallons of water compared to chemically grown cotton. Organic cotton also produces fewer greenhouse gases and uses 62% less energy compared to chemically grown cotton.



Made Safe Factories

We adopted our Code of Conduct in 1998 and have ever since worked with suppliers who actively pursue freedom of association, a good working environment, legal salaries and benefits, legal working hours, real employment, human treatment and compliance with the national laws that exist in production countries.
